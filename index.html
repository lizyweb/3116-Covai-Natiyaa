Covai Natiyaa Kalakshetra

Covai Natiyaa Academy, founded by Dr.S.Alia Nisha in 2004, is a leading institution dedicated to preserving and promoting classical dance forms. With over 20 years of excellence, we offer certification courses in Bharatanatyam, Mohiniattam, Kathak, and more, graded by Annamalai University. Our academy has expanded to 8 branches and nearly 3000 students have graduated. We proudly offer free classes to transgenders and housewives, making the arts accessible to all. At Covai Natiyaa Academy, we nurture talent, celebrate diversity, and inspire through dance.
